Size Guide

Choosing the right size helps you get the most from your apparel and reduces the chance of returns. Many apparel sites still fail to provide adequate sizing information, leaving users without the details they need. We’ve created an easy‑to‑use guide with conventional, numeric and measurement details so you can order with confidence.

Size Numeric Chest (in) Chest (cm) Waist (in) Waist (cm)
XS 0–2 30–32 76–81 24–26 61–66
S 4–6 34–36 86–91 28–30 71–76
M 8–10 38–40 97–102 32–34 81–86
L 12–14 42–44 107–112 36–38 91–97
XL 16–18 46–48 117–122 40–42 102–107
XXL 20–22 50–52 127–132 44–46 112–117

Measurements refer to body circumference. If you fall between sizes, consider sizing up for a more relaxed fit. Providing both conventional and numeric sizing helps users compare with their usual size, which is critical because sizes often vary widely among brands.
